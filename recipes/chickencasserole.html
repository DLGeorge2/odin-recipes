The Best Chicken Casserole

Tender chicken topped with ritz crackers make for a gooey goodness the whole family can enjoy!

Making this delicious dish is a snap. All you need is:

  • 4 cups cooked chicken (fully cooked and shredded)
  • 10.5 oz cream of chicken soup
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 1/8 teaspon pepper (or to taste)
  • 1 sleeve Ritz crackers (crushed)
  • 6 tablespoons butter (melted)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F. Prepare a casserole dish with non-stick cooking spray and set aside.
  2. Mix cooked and shredded chicken with cream of chicken soup, sour cream and seasonings. Add to the bottom of your prepared dish.
  3. TOp your casserole with crushed crackers and melted butter. Bake for 20-30 minutes
