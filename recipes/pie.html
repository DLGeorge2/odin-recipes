The Best Peanut Butter Pie

Pie that will make the perfect finish to any meal

This pie is a breeze to make and only takes a few minutes to prepare.

Here's what you'll need:

  • 1 9 inch prepared graham cracker crust
  • 1 8 ounce package of cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 of creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 of confectioners' sugar
  • 1 16 ounce container of frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 15 minature chocolated covered peanut butter cups, unwrapped

Directions

  1. Mix the cream cheese, confectioners' sugar and peanut butter together until smooth. Fold in 1/2 of the whipped topping. Spoon mixture into the cracker crust
  2. Place the remaining whipped topping over the top of the peanut butter mixture and garnish with the peanut butter cups. Chill for at least 2 hours or overnight before serving
